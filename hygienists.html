Morgan Slippy, RDH


Hi! I’m Morgan. I’m originally from Idaho and earned my Bachelor’s degree in Dental Hygiene from Pacific University. I moved to Seattle in 2022 to continue growing in my career while exploring everything the city has to offer.

As the quietest member of our hygiene team, I bring a calm and gentle presence to each appointment. I know that dental visits can feel overwhelming for some patients, so I focus on creating a comfortable, relaxed experience while providing thorough, high-quality care.

Outside the office, I enjoy soaking up sunshine, going to the gym, getting lost in a good book, and exploring Seattle’s happy hour and brunch spots. I’m also obsessed with my dog Oakley; he’s been by my side since I was 16, and I can’t resist sharing his pictures or stories whenever I get the chance.

I look forward to helping you feel confident in your smile and leaving with one on your face!

      Holistic Hygiene

      Edith Serna, MS, RDH


      Hello, I'm Edith! I am a Yakima native and earned my bachelor's degree in biology from the University of Washington before completing dental hygiene school at Pima Medical Institute (the building right next door!). I later pursued my master's degree in microbiology and cell science because I truly love learning and understanding how the body works at a deeper level.

      I am especially passionate about the connection between oral health and whole-body health. My goal is to empower patients with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions about their care, while always providing thorough and gentle treatment. I believe patients deserve to understand the why behind what we do, and I love helping translate the science into something practical and meaningful.

      When I'm not in the office, I enjoy spending time with my parents, cooking and baking recipes from all over the world, painting, reading, exercising, and making my home a beautiful space to be.
