Whole-Body Wellness

Biological Dentistry

At Integrative Dentistry, we believe your oral health is deeply connected to your total well-being. By blending advanced modern dentistry with natural, biological principles, we treat the mouth and body as one interconnected system. Our goal is to help you make safe, informed decisions that support your entire body, spirit, and smile.

For over 30 years, our Seattle practice has been at the forefront of biological dentistry. We partner with a network of like-minded healthcare professionals to ensure biocompatibility, provide nutritional support, and guide systemic detoxification.

We perform dentistry in harmony with your body’s natural systems. Through advanced compatibility testing, we identify individual sensitivities to avoid any adverse reactions. We have proudly been a mercury-free and fluoride-free practice since 1987.

Because our foundation is built on whole-body wellness, our care extends far beyond conventional treatments. We look at the bigger picture, evaluating how your dental health intersects with your airway, sleep quality, TMJ function, posture, and cranial balance.

Advanced Care

Our Office & Technology

We’ve created a soothing, ultra-clean space designed specifically with our chemically and environmentally sensitive (MCS) patients in mind. By utilizing advanced air and water filtration, strictly low-VOC materials, and a practice-wide fragrance-free policy, we ensure a safe, reaction-free experience for everyone who walks through our doors.

  • CEREC® Same-Day Crowns Advanced CAD technology to design and mill bio-compatible custom crowns in a single visit.
  • 3D Galileos Cone Beam Comprehensive assessment of teeth, jaws, sinuses, and airway.
  • Digital Diagnostics Low-radiation digital x-rays eliminate toxic developing chemicals.
  • Ozone Therapy Medical ozone naturally eliminates bacteria and supports healing.
  • Targeted Laser Therapies Minimally invasive laser gum treatments and infrared cellular healing.
